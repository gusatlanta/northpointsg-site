About NorthPoint

Recruiting Built on
Relationships, Not Résumés

NorthPoint Search Group is a highly reputable full-service recruiting firm dedicated to sourcing and placing top-tier professionals across multiple industries. Since 2005, our award-winning process and dedicated team have made us one of the leading recruitment firms in the Southeast.

We believe in fostering strong partnerships built on trust, integrity, and mutual success. Our approach is deeply rooted in consultation — we take the time to understand the unique requirements and culture of each client before we ever send a résumé.

Based in Atlanta and serving clients across the nation, our specialized divisions cover Accounting & Finance, Legal, Construction, Marketing, Healthcare Finance, Administrative, and HR & Recruiting.

How It Works

From Brief to Hired
in Four Clear Steps

No black boxes. You'll know exactly where your search stands at every stage.

1

Discovery Call

We learn your business, role, culture, and success metrics — going far beyond the job description.

2

Tell Us Your Needs

After the call we have a clear picture of your requirements, timeline, and must-have qualities.

3

Vetted Shortlist

You receive a curated shortlist of 3–5 pre-interviewed, confirmed-interested candidates — no résumé flood.

4

Close & Onboard

We manage offer negotiation, counteroffers, and stay engaged through the first 90 days to protect your investment.
